FAQ

What is the easiest way to get started with the Binary.com API?

View the Code Samples. You can use these code snippets in your apps to open a connection to the Binary.com WebSocket API service.

What is the easiest way to build my own Binary.com front-end application?

Simply copy our open-source code, and adapt it to meet your needs. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Open a GitHub account
  2. Download the GitHub Desktop application
  3. Fork one of Binary.com's front-end repositories, such as Binary Webtrader (view app) or any of the other open-source projects listed here
  4. Make your code changes in your fork
  5. Publish your fork using the GitHub Pages facility
  6. For SSL and website acceleration for your app, please open a free account on Cloudflare

Will I earn commissions if users sign up to Binary.com using my app?

Yes. Please follow these steps:

  1. Sign up as an affiliate
  2. Insert your affiliate token into the new_account_virtual call in your app

How else can I earn commission or revenue?

You can:

  1. Sign up as a payment agent to process local payments for Binary.com clients in your country. You may automate your payment agent facility using the paymentagent_transfer API call.
  2. Sell your app on the Binary.com Shop. You will earn 80% of the revenue for every app sold on your behalf through our e-commerce site.
  3. If you are prepared for your app to offer higher contract prices than the main Binary.com website, you may add a markup percentage when you register your app. This markup percentage is a percentage of contract payouts, and is added to all contract prices in your app. At every month-end, the aggregate markup is paid to you. Please sign up as an affiliate and contact your affiliate manager to learn more about this facility.

Do you provide Omnibus account facilities?

Please read about our Omnibus account facilities.

Can someone build an app for me?

Sure! You can hire developers on sites such as UpWork. Just look for developers who are familiar with JavaScript and WebSocket technology.
